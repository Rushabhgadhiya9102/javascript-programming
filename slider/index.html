Alaska moose

The Alaska moose, or Alaskan moose in Alaska, or giant moose and Yukon moose in Canada, is a subspecies of moose that ranges from Alaska to western Yukon. The Alaska moose is the largest subspecies of moose.

Moose (elk)

The moose (Alces alces), also known as elk in some regions, is the largest species of the deer family Cervidae. They are distinct for their towering size, long legs, and unique features, including a large, drooping nose and a dewlap known as a bell.

African Bush
Elephant

The African bush elephant (Loxodonta africana), also known as the African savanna elephant, is the largest land animal on Earth and is found in various habitats across sub-Saharan Africa, including savannas, forests, and even deserts.

Jaguar

The jaguar (Panthera onca) is a large cat species native to the Americas, recognized as the largest of South America's big cats and the third largest cat species worldwide.
